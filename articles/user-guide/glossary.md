# Glossary

## Appendix 1 - Definitions

### Project and Campaign terms

#### Project

- Contains one to multiple [*Campaign*](#campaign)s with a shared
  purpose/objective (e.g. monitoring of a Marine Park, a bioregional
  study).

- [*Project*](#id__1a4bxgjenifp) is a unique identifier and the name
  should be carefully chosen (e.g. “MarineParkMonitoring” is not a good
  [*Project*](#id__1a4bxgjenifp) name but “Houtman Abrolhos Reef
  Observation Areas long-term monitoring” is a great
  [*Project*](#id__1a4bxgjenifp) name)

- Sampling and image analysis methods are generally standardised within
  a [*Project*](#id__1a4bxgjenifp) but sampling method for each
  [*Campaign*](#campaign) can be different (e.g. one
  [*Campaign*](#campaign) could use DOV whereas another
  [*Campaign*](#campaign) could use BRUV or UVC).

#### Scale

- The geographic extent at which the dataset, Project, or
  [*sample*](#sample) is relevant or intended to be applied
  (e.g. International, National, Regional, or Local).

#### Type

- The category that best describes the primary context or intended use
  of the dataset or project.

- Cultural, Academic, Industry or Management

#### Campaign

- A discrete set (temporal and spatial) of [*samples*](#sample).

- All [*samples*](#sample) within a [*Campaign*](#campaign) use the same
  sampling and image analysis [*methods*](#method).

#### CampaignID

- A unique identifier for a [*Campaign*](#campaign) made up of
  YYYY-MM_Campaign_Method.

- The date is the start date of the [*Campaign*](#campaign)
  (e.g. 2023-12_Rottnest-Island-Marine-Parks_stereo-BRUVs).

#### Annotation Set

- A collection of annotations from a defined set of [*samples*](#sample)
  within a [*Campaign*](#campaign) , grouped together as a single
  dataset.

#### Annotation Metadata

- The descriptive information associated with an [*Annotation
  Set*](#annotation-set) that provides context for the annotations and
  information on how the [*samples*](#sample) were annotated.

- For example levels of identification and software used to annotate.

#### Method

- Any sampling [*method*](#method) (e.g. stereo-BRUVs, stereo-DOVs,
  UVC). Contact Administrator to create a new Method.  
  Defined in the “[*Methods*](#method)” tab.

#### Sample

- Single observational unit (e.g. a BRUV deployment or DOV transect).  
  Must match exactly across sample metadata and any annotation/EMObs
  files.  
  Defined by opcode only, period only, or opcode + period.

#### Vocabularies

- The approved controlled vocabularies used in EMObs to standardise
  annotation of fish, benthic biota, and substrates.

- These ensure annotations are applied consistently and can be compared
  across [*projects*](#projects) and datasets.

### Synthesis terms

#### Synthesis/Syntheses

- Combines information from multiple [*Campaigns*](#campaign) with the
  same [*method*](#method) from multiple locations and/or multiple
  times.

#### Scale

- The geographic extent at which the [*Synthesis*](#synthesis) is
  relevant or intended to be applied

- International, National, Regional or Local

#### Type

- The category that best describes the primary context or intended use
  of the [*Synthesis*](#synthesis).

- Cultural, Academic, Industry or Management

### User Access and Sharing terms

#### Custodian

- A [*user*](#user) who creates a [*Project*](#project), and the
  [*Campaigns*](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1yU-zrEIwBN1B-w-rnwxRlZ8rEioJ37bxk7l5LkXhgoM/edit?userstoinvite=annika.leunig%40marineecology.io&sharingaction=manageaccess&role=reader&tab=t.0#heading=h.watfbpgrrufl)
  within it, is the Custodian.

- Only the Custodian can upload
  [*Campaigns*](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1yU-zrEIwBN1B-w-rnwxRlZ8rEioJ37bxk7l5LkXhgoM/edit?userstoinvite=annika.leunig%40marineecology.io&sharingaction=manageaccess&role=reader&tab=t.0#heading=h.watfbpgrrufl)
  to that
  [*Project*](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1yU-zrEIwBN1B-w-rnwxRlZ8rEioJ37bxk7l5LkXhgoM/edit?userstoinvite=annika.leunig%40marineecology.io&sharingaction=manageaccess&role=reader&tab=t.0#heading=h.l7x8x11101qp)
  and share the
  [*Campaigns*](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1yU-zrEIwBN1B-w-rnwxRlZ8rEioJ37bxk7l5LkXhgoM/edit?userstoinvite=annika.leunig%40marineecology.io&sharingaction=manageaccess&role=reader&tab=t.0#heading=h.watfbpgrrufl)
  or the entire
  [*Project*](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1yU-zrEIwBN1B-w-rnwxRlZ8rEioJ37bxk7l5LkXhgoM/edit?userstoinvite=annika.leunig%40marineecology.io&sharingaction=manageaccess&role=reader&tab=t.0#heading=h.l7x8x11101qp)
  with other [*Users*](#user).

- The Custodian should be the person within the institution with a long
  term interest in the data and the person who should be contacted if
  another user wants to use the data (e.g. will more likely be a
  Principal Investigator than a research assistant).

#### Projects

- [*Projects*](#project) are typically to organise data within a
  research group/organisation (e.g. Monitoring of a specific marine park
  by the NSW Department of Primary Industries).

- Sharing a
  [*Project*](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1yU-zrEIwBN1B-w-rnwxRlZ8rEioJ37bxk7l5LkXhgoM/edit?userstoinvite=annika.leunig%40marineecology.io&sharingaction=manageaccess&role=reader&tab=t.0#heading=h.l7x8x11101qp)
  with another [*User*](#user) allows them to query all
  [*Campaigns*](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1yU-zrEIwBN1B-w-rnwxRlZ8rEioJ37bxk7l5LkXhgoM/edit?userstoinvite=annika.leunig%40marineecology.io&sharingaction=manageaccess&role=reader&tab=t.0#heading=h.watfbpgrrufl)
  within it.

#### Synthesis

- A [*Synthesis*](http://synthesis/Syntheses) is designed for sharing of
  data from multiple
  [*Campaigns*](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1yU-zrEIwBN1B-w-rnwxRlZ8rEioJ37bxk7l5LkXhgoM/edit?userstoinvite=annika.leunig%40marineecology.io&sharingaction=manageaccess&role=reader&tab=t.0#heading=h.watfbpgrrufl)
  for data analysis.

- The User who created the [*Synthesis*](http://synthesis/Syntheses) is
  the Custodian of the [*Synthesis*](http://synthesis/Syntheses).

- Only the Custodian can share the
  [*Synthesis*](http://synthesis/Syntheses) with other Users.

#### User

- User registration and login provides secure access to the
  [*Campaigns*](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1yU-zrEIwBN1B-w-rnwxRlZ8rEioJ37bxk7l5LkXhgoM/edit?userstoinvite=annika.leunig%40marineecology.io&sharingaction=manageaccess&role=reader&tab=t.0#heading=h.watfbpgrrufl)
  and [*Projects*](#project) you upload and that are shared with you.
